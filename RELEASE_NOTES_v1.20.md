# v1.20 – Full-width categories and product drag ordering

- Cashier category strip now spans the full cashier screen width above products and cart.
- Category strip design was simplified for touch use and hides the native scrollbar.
- Product tile colors are stable and no longer change when the product order changes.
- Product ordering panel supports direct touch/mouse drag reordering inside the category ordering grid.
- Existing arrow and "Na začátek" controls remain available as a fallback.

No Supabase migration is required.
